The Details

 

Code of Conduct

Our conference is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form. Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate for any conference venue, including talks, workshops, parties, Twitter, and other online media. Conference participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the conference without a refund at the discretion of the conference organizers.

Panel Day - Thursday, June 11th

There will be a Hallway Track Meeting for socialization at the beginning and the end of the day and a Panel Webinar for holding the main event.

We are using a single password on all our online meetings and webinars. The password is "fgd2020".

Hallway Track Meeting

There will be community leaders heading up rooms in this Zoom Meeting allowing people to break out into discussion and meet one and another.

Panel Webinar

This session will include short talks, panel discussions, and lightning talks. There will be no breaks in this session and we will be recording so we can release it later. Please use the Q&A tool to ask questions as well as upvote and comment on questions from the community.

 

Hands-On Day - Friday, June 12th

There will be workshops, discussions, and presentations going on in three rooms. Those that have a ticket for these sessions will be able to enter the appropriate meeting and then be placed in the correct break out room. We will have people helping out in the main rooms to help out.

Virtual desktops will be set up for those signed up for the QGIS course, you will be receiving an email with your credentials from AWS. Please download the client before Friday. The virtual machines will be spun down until Friday.

The password for all the meetings is "fgd2020".
